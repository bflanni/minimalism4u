Becoming a minimalist in no small task. But by changing one small area in your life at a time, it can be accomplished. It is truly all about balance.

Meditation can change your life. By starting with 5 minutes out of your day to clear your mind, it can change your whole mindset. It takes practice to clear your mind of all thoughts, but once accomplished, a sense of calm comes over you. It allows you to then think more clearly.

Minimalism is about simplifying your life. This also includes decluttering your home. A clean, oranized home allows for more space physically and mentally. When a home is messy it can put up mental blocks. A book that can help with that is The life-changing magic of tidyng up by Marie Kondo.

Becoming a minimalist doesnt mean you are starving yourself. It means eathing healthier and making better meal choices. Cutting processed foods, eating more vegetables, Cutting back on sugar and drinking more water. Some might want to look into intermittent fasting. Intermittent fasting is only having a small window and for the rest of the day you are drinking water, herbal tea or black coffee. This is easier than it sounds.
